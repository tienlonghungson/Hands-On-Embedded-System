The program "Quick_Test" is a short program to test the general functionality of the TLE9832.
It is written for the Infineon TLE9834_EVB board.

MON1 push-button: LS1/2 toggling
MON2 push-button: HS1/2 toggling
MON3 push-button: HALL_Supply toggling
MON4 push-button: LS1/2 + HALL_Supply + HS1/2 toggling
