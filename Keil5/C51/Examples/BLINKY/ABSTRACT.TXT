The BLINKY project is the example program used in User's
Guide: MCBx51 Evaluation Board - Chapter 5 Programming.
 
It does nothing more than blink the LEDs on the MCBx51 
evaluation board.           


The Blinky program is available in different targets:
  Simulator:       Blinky example in SMALL model for Simulation

  MCBx51: 8xC51Rx: Blinky example for the MCBx51 Eval Board
                   running on 80C51Rx+, 80C51RD2, or compatible
